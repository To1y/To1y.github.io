Project Idea

One of the biggest barriers faced by many people these days(atleast in the first world) is not lack of resources or access to information. It is a sense of demoralization and learned helplessness. Many people doubt themselves so much that they not only give up mid-way through their ideas but they avoid starting them entirely. My project idea is quite simple but purposefully so, as to be within my technical limits.
It is quite straight-forward, the idea is a sort of life-hack based on the premise of "self-talk" People who doubt themselves, have issues with self-esteem tend to consistently be overly self-critical in their internal monologue. As we humans are creatures of habit, we are victims to repetition, regardless if it is good or bad, the brain likes to follow familiar pathways.
The idea is an application that will flash the user 'positive words'(words that inspire productivity, confidence) at a rapid rate not giving them enough time to be critical, basically overstimulating their mind with positive words at such a rate that it does not let it default into its normal negative pattern. It is as much an experiment as a self-help tool as I'm not sure how effective it will be. Perhaps it will do nothing, perhaps users will develop an aversion to it, or just as likely it will work, but only for users who are already (getting) better. However I believe with habitual use over time it may help some users develop healthy 'self-talk' habits(i.e. not critisizing one-self needlessly.)
